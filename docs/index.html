About Us

We Are
HOST
Eindhoven

HOST is a community for international students that aims to help international students to feel at home in Eindhoven. At HOST you can expect hospitality, to build friendships, and to be encouraged to meet God. We organize various formal and informal events, focussed on helping Christian international students grow in their faith and share experiences. These events also include activities that are a lot of fun, and involve cultural exchange. However, you donâ€™t have to be a Christian to come along; we welcome all students regardless of their cultural background or religion!


HOST Eindhoven is part of IFES (International Fellowship of Evangelical Students), an organization supporting ministries to international students in cities all over the globe, among which 20 cities in the Netherlands. Furthermore, IFES Netherlands organizes a number of activities where you can meet many international students studying in different cities in the Netherlands.

Weekly Meetings

Our main meetings are on Friday evenings, when we meet for dinner and a Bible study! Every second week it is at Dommelstraat 6 in Eindhoven city center, and every other week we meet in small groups at various locations in Eindhoven.

Family Project

Would you like to have regular meetings with a Dutch family over a meal in their home? We match international students up with a host family either individually or in pairs so that you can learn more about Dutch culture in a family setting.
